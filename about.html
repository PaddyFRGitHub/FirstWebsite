Education

I attended school at The Misbourne Academy from 2011 - 2016, and achieved 5 GCSE's A* - C. These were in Maths, English Language, English Literature, Philosophy and Ethic and ICT. After that, I attended Aylesbury College between 2016 - 2018 where I studied IT and achieved Merit Merit Pass which then allowed me to progress to university. I enrolled at De Montfort University, Leicester, studying Computer Science. I engaged in modules such as mobile applciation development, object oriented programming, agile team development, and web application development, using both front and back-end technologies. I am currently in my final year and due to finish May 2023, with 2:1 prospects.

Hobbies & Interests

My hobbies and interests include; snowboarding which I have had a keen interest in for many years and has allowed me to join the Snowsports society at university, where I have won numerous competitions. Kickboxing, which I have trained in for a number of years, reaching a high belt and competing in regional competitions throughout.

Personal Life

I was born in High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire and have lived there my entire adult life, with the exception of a 5 month period where I worked in a kitchen in La Plagne, in the French alps. After that I returned home, and attended university, living in Leicester during the term time, and at home during the holidays.
